Power Forward

Just runs up and down the court, occassionally touches the ball.

The description above pretty much says it all, you barely touch the ball. I have really not cared much for this position, nor will I start caring for this position, or ever play this position but they are just basically Mini-Centers and Big Forwards.

Roles and Responsibilities

You athleticism combined with your speed allows you to outrebound opposing frontcourt players by outhustling Centers and outsizing Small Forwards. Your skillset can range from dominating the paint area around the basket and being able to stretch out the defense and spread out the offense by becoming an outside shooter for the team. This provides more space for driving lanes of forwards and guards who can attack the basket far better than you do. If you are good at scoring, they will pass you the ball when you are in a position to score or during a mismatch. However, usually, the main responsibility of this position is to be the secondary help defender in the painted area by assisting the center.

I should have more about this position but really they are there to set screens, defend, rebound, and occasionally score. Nowadays, Small Forwards fill this position since it is so useless.
