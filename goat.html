About

Lebron James is the greatest player ever to play the sport of basketball. I know a lot of people dont think that way, but hey its my website. He is a 6'8 Small Forward currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers in his 16th season in the NBA. During his tenure, he has solidified himself on the Mount Rushmore of the basketball world by being a 3x NBA champion, a 4x NBA Most Valuable Player, appeared in 15x NBA All-Star Games, won Rookie of the Year back in 2004, NBA Scoring Leader in 2008, a 3x AP Athelete of the Year, a 2x Sports Illustrated Sportsperson of the Year , a 2x Olympic Gold Medalist, oh and he is starring on the long awaited sequel Space Jam 2 which will feature him with Looney Tunes characters on a basketball team.

Basketball Accomplishments

The NBA is has been around ever since 1946. June of 2019 marks the 73rd year of the NBA league. Basketball itself has been around for 127 years. So it is safe to say, a lot of people played it already, both in the professional level and the amateur level. Historically, the best team with the most athletic and skilled individuals come out as champions in basketball. These players train themselves throughout the course of their career, they get coached by multitude of people, and play alongside other great individuals. Enter the 18 year old Lebron James, straight out of High School, being deemed as "The Chosen One" for being great at basketball without any professional background from his parents or his coaches. His Dad was in and out of prison, and he was raised by a single parent who had troubles raising him as a child. Come into the NBA, rookies usually tend to struggle in their early years trying to adjust to the new environment. Even James struggled a bit, however in 2007, a undermanned Cleveland Cavaliers team led by a rookie coach, with no other All-Star in the team than James, made it to the NBA finals. He simply crushed the opposing teams to get there but only to lose to the San Antonio Spurs in a 0-4 series. Although a sweep, he still single-handedly brought his team to the NBA Finals which is quite a feat for the 21 year old James at that time.

He then moved to the Miami Heat to play alongside Dwyane Wade, another hall of fame All-Star off guard during the summer of 2010. The basketball world hated him for his decision as it was a move saying that he was not good enough to win and lead a team by himself. They would eventually win back-to-back championships during their time there but the public did not consider those as major accomplishments as he needed to go to a "super-team" to do so. He was hated by the people of Cleveland for leaving the team, a place he considered home as he was born and grew up there. Despite the resentment of the people, James still decided to come back to Cleveland during the summer of 2014, with the goal of winning a championship for his hometown. All doubters and naysayers were silenced when he delivered Cleveland Cavaliers their 1st NBA Championship in franchise history defeating the Golden State Warriors in the NBA Finals of 2016 after being down 3 games to 1 in the series alongside Kyrie Irving, J.R. Smith, Kevin Love, and Tristan Thompson.

Lebron James became the first player in the history of the game to lead both teams, yes both Golden State Warriors and the Cleveland Cavaliers, in Points, Rebounds, Assists, Steals, and Blocks. Those categories are pretty much what matters in the stats column in this sport.

Conclusion

Obviously, his basketball accomplishments are already eye opening. But it does not stop there, he recently had a Twitter feud with President Donald Trump when he spoke out his political opinions to the public. This dude also opened a school called the "I Promise School" for the children of Akron, Ohio which guarantees free college tuition to graduates if they are able to maintain a certain GPA throughout their studies. He has a net worth of 500 million dollars as of 2019 and owns or is a shareholder for multiple businesses such as MLB teams, soccer teams, Blaze Pizza, production company called "SpringHill Entertainment", and media company "Uninterrupted". But for me, the reason why he is the G.O.A.T. is simply because he overcame the odds that were stacked against him from the day he was born. Once he reached the NBA, he did not just stop there. He put himself within the discussion of being the greatest player to play the game and eventually over took the title in the eyes of many from Michael Jordan, star of the initial movie Space Jam. His life is just a testament that hard work pays off no matter what you do in life and how much the odds are stacked against you.
